Family Court



Family courts are specialized courts that handle cases related to family matters, including divorce, child custody, adoption, domestic violence, matrimonial disputes, and other issues concerning family relationships.

Here are some general details about family courts in India:



Jurisdiction:

Family courts have jurisdiction over specific areas or districts.

They typically handle cases related to family matters within their designated jurisdiction.



Structure:

Family courts operate under the overall judicial system of the respective state or union territory in India.

They may have their own designated buildings or be housed within the premises of the district court.



Case Types:


Family courts deal with a wide range of cases, including:



  • Divorce and separation
  • Child custody and visitation rights
  • Adoption and guardianship
  • Domestic violence and protection orders
  • Maintenance and alimony
  • Property disputes within families
  • Mediation and counseling for resolving family disputes
