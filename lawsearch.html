CRIMINAL LAW



Criminal law refers to a body of laws that apply to criminal acts. This area of law deals with offences that are considered to be harmful to society as a whole, such as murder,theft, and assault. There are two types of criminal laws: misdemeanors and felonies.

CIVIL LAW



Civil law is a system of law or body of rules dealing with the civil or private rights of citizens and not the public at large. It is primarily concerned with the private relations between two or more citizens and is generally codified. This area of law deals with disputes between individuals, such as contract disputes or personal injury lawsuits.
