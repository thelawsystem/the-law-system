District Courts

District courts are the lower courts in the judicial system of India that operate at the district level.

Each district in India usually has its own district court.

As for the Madras High Court jurisdiction, which covers the state of Tamil Nadu and the Union Territory of Puducherry, there are several district courts functioning under its authority.


The district courts in Tamil Nadu are subordinate to the jurisdiction of the Madras High Court.

They handle various types of civil and criminal cases within their respective districts.

Some of the prominent district courts in Tamil Nadu include:



  • Chennai District Court
  • Coimbatore District Court
  • Madurai District Court
  • Tiruchirappalli District Court
  • Salem District Court
  • Erode District Court
  • Tirunelveli District Court
  • Vellore District Court
  • Thanjavur District Court
  • Thoothukudi District Court


Each district court operates under the supervision of a District Judge or Principal District Judge who oversees the functioning of the court and the judicial proceedings within the district.
